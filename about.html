About Sri Vedha Ayurveda

Speaking about Sri Vedha Ayurveda After consulting patients in my offline settings in Bangalore and Gulbarga, my patients were pleased with the results and requested that I adopt the modern technological trend of offering online consultation options. Prior to this, I was planning a consultation portal but after running into some problems during follow-up consultation, I decided to launch a website.

What Sri Vedha Ayurveda means In Sanskrit, the word Sri denotes brightness, good fortune, and prosperity. I chose the auspicious name Vedha from the Ram Raksha Stotra since Prabhu Shree Rama is my Ishta devta. Vedha refers to a surgical procedure in Ayurveda that involves rupturing or puncturing the wound. My clinic's offline operations will soon begin.

Vaidya Saishakthi

Vaidya Saishakthi is a licensed and experienced ayurvedic physician who adheres to real shastrokta ayurveda in the pursuit of whole body, mind, and soul healing.

The famed Taranath government Ayurveda Medical College and Hospital in Bellary, Karnataka (Rajiv Gandhi University of health sciences) is where he received his Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) degree.

He fervently adheres to the therapeutic tenets professed by his mentor, Dr. Shivanand Karigar, M.D. (Ayu), a pupil of Dr. Ishwar Reddy.

Like his Guru, Dr. Saishakthi practices Eka moolika prayoga (single drug therapy) and has a keen interest in Dravyaguna and its siddhant in clinical practice.

Patients with a range of chronic conditions relating to neurology, male infertility, rheumatology, dermatology, and other lifestyle illnesses have been treated by him.He provides a tailored diet and way of living.
